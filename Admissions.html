Admission Requirements

Undergraduate Requirements

  • Completed Secondary Education
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0
  • English Proficiency Test
  • Two Letters of Recommendation
  • Personal Statement

Graduate Requirements

  • Bachelor's Degree
  • Minimum GPA of 3.5
  • GRE/GMAT Scores
  • Research Proposal
  • Professional Experience

Application Process

Step 1: Online Application

Complete the online application form and pay the application fee

Step 2: Document Submission

Submit all required documents including transcripts and certificates

Step 3: Interview

Selected candidates will be invited for an interview

Step 4: Admission Decision

Receive admission decision within 4-6 weeks

Scholarships and Financial Aid

Merit Scholarships

Available for students with outstanding academic performance

  • Full tuition coverage
  • Partial tuition coverage
  • Academic excellence awards

Need-Based Aid

Financial assistance based on economic circumstances

  • Work-study programs
  • Payment plans
  • Emergency funds
