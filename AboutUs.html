Our History

Established in 1970, University of Arusha has grown from a small college to a prestigious institution of higher learning. Over the decades, we have maintained our commitment to academic excellence and innovation while adapting to meet the changing needs of our students and society.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide quality education that transforms students into competent professionals and responsible citizens who contribute to global development.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of academic excellence in Africa, fostering innovation, research, and sustainable development.
